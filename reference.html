Data We Used from NASA:

  • NASA Apollo 11 Passive Seismic Experiment -
    Link: https://moon.nasa.gov/resources/13/apollo-11-seismic-experiment/
    Data we found: seismic wave(strength, duration, direction), moons internal structure

  • Catalog of Lunar Seismic Data from Apollo Passive Seismic Experiment on 8-mm Video Cassette (Exabyte) Tapes -
    Link:https://nssdc.gsfc.nasa.gov/misc/documents/b53211.pdf
    Data we found: nakamura data, passive seismic data

  • Full Apollo EASEP and ALSEP descriptions -
    Link: https://history.nasa.gov/alsj/HamishALSEP.html
    Data we found:
    1. Lunar Surface Magnetometer (LSM, Exp. S-034) -> magnetic field of the Moon with time, variation of magnetic field in terms of amplitude, frequency, and direction.
    2. Lunar Passive Seismic Experiment (PSE, Exp. S-031) -> strength, duration, and approximate direction of the seismic event.
    3. Lunar Active Seismic Experiment (ASE, Exp. S-033) -> lunar seismic velocity, scale and source of energy.
